+ I am not a computer or electronics student can i learn IoT?

Absolutely, anyone can learn IoT regardless of their educational background! IoT combines concepts from various fields like programming, electronics, data analytics, and networking, but you don't need to master everything at once

+ Do I need programming knowledge to work with IoT?

Basic programming knowledge is helpful but not mandatory to get started. Many IoT platforms (like Arduino and Raspberry Pi) have beginner-friendly tutorials and ready-to-use libraries that simplify programming. Additionally, tools like Node-RED allow visual programming for IoT projects.

Yes, IoT has immense future scope! The Internet of Things is transforming industries and daily life at an unprecedented pace, and its potential continues to grow with advancements in technology.
